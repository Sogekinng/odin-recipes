Parmesan Chicken Bake

This Parmesan-style chicken bake is done casserole style (so, no breading or frying!), but still offers up that irresistible combination of tender chicken, crunchy-cheesy coating, and flavorful sauce.The first time I made this easy baked chicken Parmesan recipe, I knew it was an instant keeper.

The Parmesan-crusted chicken is perfectly crispy on the outside and lusciously juicy on the inside, and the cheese topping is hot, bubbly, and every bit as satisfying as it should be.

(Love crispy chicken and cheese? Don’t miss Baked Chicken Cordon Bleu.)

The best part?

Unlike most restaurant versions, which are deep-fried and heavy with greasy breadcrumbs and cheese, my version is baked, has minimal prep, and can be ready in 30 minutes (like my Chicken Piccata and Chicken Milanese). I’d venture to say that this is the BEST chicken parmesan recipe ever.

    Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed and finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • 6 skinless,boneless chicken breasth halves
  • 2 cups prepared marianara sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmsean cheese, divided
  • 1 (5 ounce) package garlic croutons

    Steps to do

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Coat the bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole dish with olive oil, and sprinkle with garlic and red pepper flakes.

  3. Arrange chicken breasts in the bottom of the dish.

  4. Pour marinara sauce over chicken. Sprinkle basil over marinara sauce, and top with 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese, followed by 1/2 of the Parmesan cheese, and croutons.

  5. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese and remaining Parmesan cheese.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese and croutons are golden brown and the chicken is no longer pink inside, about 35 minutes to an hour, depending on the shape and thickness of your chicken breasts. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the thickest part of a chicken breast should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
